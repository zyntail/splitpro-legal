Privacy Policy for SplitPro

Effective Date: May 2026

SplitPro is an expense splitting and spend analysis app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the SplitPro mobile app.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Account information such as name, email address, and profile photo when you sign in using email/password or Google Sign-In.
  • Group and expense information such as group names, member names, expense titles, amounts, categories, payer details, split details, balances, and settlement information.
  • App usage and diagnostic information needed to maintain, secure, and improve the app.
  • Subscription-related status information if you purchase premium features through Google Play Billing.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your SplitPro account.
  • Allow users to create groups, add expenses, calculate balances, and simplify settlements.
  • Show spend analysis, summaries, and category-wise insights.
  • Provide AI-powered spend insights if you use premium AI features.
  • Maintain app security, prevent abuse, and improve app performance.
  • Manage subscription access if premium features are enabled.

3. AI Features

SplitPro may provide AI-powered spend analysis features. When AI features are used, the app may process summarized expense data such as total spend, category breakdowns, member-wise balances, and spending trends.

We do not send payment credentials, passwords, or unnecessary personal information to AI services. AI-generated insights are for informational purposes only and should not be considered financial advice.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share limited data with trusted service providers only as needed to operate the app, including:

  • Firebase and Google Cloud services for authentication, database storage, hosting, backend functions, and analytics.
  • Google Play services for subscription and billing management.
  • AI service providers for generating spend insights when you use AI features.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your app data is stored using Firebase and Google Cloud infrastructure. We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including authentication, access controls, and backend security rules.

However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

6. User-Generated Content

Users may create shared group and expense content. Group members may be able to view expense information added by other members in the same group.

Please do not enter sensitive information such as passwords, bank account numbers, card numbers, or private identification numbers in expense descriptions or group names.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information as long as needed to provide the app's features, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and improve the service.

You may request deletion of your account or data by contacting us.

8. Children's Privacy

SplitPro is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Your Choices

You can:

  • Sign out of the app.
  • Request account or data deletion.
  • Stop using premium or AI features.
  • Contact us for privacy-related questions.
