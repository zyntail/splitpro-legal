Account and Data Deletion

SplitPro allows users to delete their account and request deletion of associated app data. This page explains how to delete your account, what data is deleted, and what data may be retained.

How to delete your account in the app

You can delete your SplitPro account directly inside the app:

SplitPro App → Profile / Settings → Delete Account

Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the deletion process.

If you cannot access the app

If you no longer have access to the app, you can request account deletion by contacting us at:

Email: zyntail.ai@gmail.com
Subject: SplitPro Account Deletion Request

Please include the email address used for your SplitPro account so we can identify and process your request.

Data that will be deleted

When your account deletion request is processed, we will delete or anonymize data associated with your account where applicable, including:

  • Account profile information, such as name and email address
  • Groups created by you, where deletion is applicable
  • Expense records associated with your account, where deletion is applicable
  • Spend analysis data associated with your account
  • AI insight data associated with your account, if any

Data that may be retained

Some data may be retained if required for legal compliance, billing records, fraud prevention, security, dispute resolution, or to preserve records shared with other group members.

Retention period

We aim to process account deletion requests within 30 days. Some backup, billing, security, or legally required records may be retained for a longer period where necessary.

Partial data deletion

You may also contact us to request deletion of specific data, such as certain groups or expenses, without deleting your entire account.

Contact

For account deletion, data deletion, or privacy-related questions, contact us at: zyntail.ai@gmail.com
